FAQ

Common Questions
Honestly Answered

How do you ensure data security and HIPAA compliance?
We operate with HIPAA-aligned workflows including encrypted data transfer, role-based access controls, and signed BAAs with every technology provider we use. No PHI is ever stored unsecured.
What accuracy levels can we realistically expect?
Our target is 98%+ coding accuracy, achieved through a structured multi-layer QA review process — not just single-pass coding. We track accuracy per batch and report it transparently.
Do you support all medical specialties?
Yes. We handle multi-specialty coding including primary care, surgery, internal medicine, cardiology, orthopedics, and more — based on your specific requirements and payer guidelines.
How quickly can we get started?
Typically 24–48 hours after initial assessment and workflow setup. We move fast — the onboarding process is designed to be low-friction so you see results quickly.
Can you handle large volumes or backlog surges?
Yes. Our scalable team model is specifically designed for backlog clearance as well as ongoing high-volume operations. We don't compromise quality as volume scales.
What does your QA process actually look like?
Every coded chart goes through a secondary QA review — we don't rely on statistical sampling. Our QA team validates code accuracy, modifier use, bundling rules, and documentation alignment before any report is finalized.
