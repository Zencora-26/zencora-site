FAQ

Common Questions
Honestly Answered

Can we engage you for just one billing stage?
Yes — many clients start with the stage where they're losing most revenue (coding QA, AR follow-up, denial management) and expand over time. No rigid packages required to begin.
How do you keep PHI secure and HIPAA compliant?
Encrypted transfers, role-based access controls, and signed BAAs with every technology partner. PHI is never stored unsecured or accessed outside authorized workflows.
What accuracy levels can we realistically expect?
We target 98%+ coding accuracy through 100% chart-level QA — not sampling. Accuracy is tracked per batch, per coder, and by code category in your weekly report.
How is your denial management different?
We do root-cause analysis on every denial pattern and fix the upstream coding or billing issue that caused it. The goal is fewer denials over time — not just faster appeal filing.
Do you cover all medical specialties?
Yes — primary care, surgery, cardiology, orthopedics, internal medicine, and more. We adapt to your specialty-specific payer mix and documentation requirements.
How quickly can we get started?
24–48 hours from initial assessment to go-live. We map to your existing EHR and PM system without requiring costly integrations or long setup phases.
Can you handle volume spikes and backlogs?
Yes. Our flex-scale team model is built for backlog clearance and surge capacity without compromising accuracy SLAs or turnaround commitments.
What's actually in your weekly report?
Coding accuracy by batch and coder, denial breakdown by category and payer, AR aging summary, clean claim rate, and revenue capture analysis — real numbers, not summary PDFs.
